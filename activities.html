Lazy Cat Island Resort

Activities

At Lazy Cat Resort, we work with tour organizations across the island to help you book tour activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, animal encounters, jungle ziplining, and historical tours.

Our friendly Tour Desk operators are available for you in the main resort lobby every day from 8am-4pm. We can provide information on the different tours, and when you are ready to decide, you can book directly with us - no hassle needed!
