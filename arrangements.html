Travel Arrangements

Most guests will fly in to A'oi regional airport, which is approximately a 20km distance from the resort. We offer free airport shuttle pickup, no reservation necessary. The shuttles depart every twenty minutes outside the airport. Look for the shuttle labeled "Lazy Cat Resort."

For any questions or difficulty locating the shuttle, please contact us at 111-222-3333.
