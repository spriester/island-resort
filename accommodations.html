Lazy Cat Island Resort

Acommodations

We offer a variety of room styles, including king room, queen room, and double two-bed room. We have one honeymoon suite available upon inquiry as well.

All rooms are equipped with WiFi, mini-fridge and coffee maker, daily maid service, and cable TVs.

Our resort amenities include two swimming pools, a hot tub/sauna, beach chairs, two restaurants, a cafe, and a gift shop/convenience store.

We are pet-friendly! Call to inquire about our pet-friendly rooms.
